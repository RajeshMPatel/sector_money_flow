How to Read CMF & RS (Click to Expand)

21-Day CMF (Chaikin Money Flow) measures Volume to track where institutional "smart money" is flowing.

  • Positive (> 0): Accumulation. Institutions are quietly buying. > +0.10 is strong, > +0.20 is extreme.
  • Negative (< 0): Distribution. Institutions are selling. < -0.10 is strong, < -0.20 is extreme.

20-Day RS % (Relative Strength) measures Price momentum compared to the S&P 500 (SPY).

  • Positive (> 0%): Outperforming the broader market.
  • Negative (< 0%): Underperforming the broader market.

Cheat Code: Watch for divergences. If a sector is crashing but CMF is positive, institutions are buying the dip. If a sector is hitting highs but CMF is negative, a trap is forming and institutions are selling into the rally.
